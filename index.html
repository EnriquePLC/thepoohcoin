About us

In 1928, A.A. The legacy

With $POOH, we aim to honor the legacy of A.A. Milne’s timeless classic and the joy it has brought to readers of all ages. As Tigger first bounded onto the scene with his irrepressible energy, $POOH leaps into the world of cryptocurrency with a heart full of nostalgia and a mission to spread positivity.

$POOH is more than just a cryptocurrency; it’s a celebration of the simple pleasures, the adventures sought in the thicket of the woods, and the lessons learned in the warmth of companionship. It’s about the laughter shared over a jar of honey and the comfort of knowing that you have friends who care.
